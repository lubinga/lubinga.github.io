For ConUHacks III my team and I created an IOT automated drink mixer using Flask and some repurposed stepper motors from a CNC machine. The project earned us the first place prize over all from a pool of 600+ students from across the country. It is also worth mentioning that we, FamingoLabs, have earned a top five spot every single time we have competed at hackathon together.
